Red Sox Rally For Late Win Over Orioles
The Boston Red Sox beat the Baltimore Orioles, 7-4, at Oriole Park at Camden Yards. The win was largely due to the efforts of right fielder L.T. Tolbert, who captured player of the game honors. He was 4 for 4 with a triple and 3 singles. He scored 2 times. Boston reliever Shae Simmons was credited with the win, to go to 2-1. Jared Hughes took the loss for Baltimore. This win makes it 5 in a row for Boston.

Tolbert had a game-changing single in the top of the eighth. On a 1-2 slider from Kyle Ryan, he banged a run-scoring single to center, giving the Red Sox a 5-4 lead. It was the 39th hit of the year for Tolbert.

"As well as we've been playing, I think we can play ever better," said Boston manager matt atwell.
