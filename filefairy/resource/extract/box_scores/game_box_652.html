A 3-RBI performance from left fielder Scott Hurst, the 22 overall pick in the 2018 draft, led the Baltimore Orioles to a 5-3 win over the Toronto Blue Jays. Hurst had a game-changing double in the top of the ninth. On a full count fastball from Steve Norris, he banged a 2-run double to right, giving the Orioles a 5-3 lead. It was the 4th double of the year for Hurst.

"Win or lose, we pick each other up in this clubhouse," Hurst told the Baltimore Herald.
