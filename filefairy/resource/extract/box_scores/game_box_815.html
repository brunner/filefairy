Multi-HR Day for Salter as Red Sox Overpower Astros, 4-3
Fans who gathered at Fenway Park saw a good contest between the Boston Red Sox and Houston Astros.

The Boston Red Sox bested the Houston Astros, 4-3, at Fenway Park. Blaise Salter was named player of the game. He was 4 for 4 with 2 home runs and a double, while scoring 3 runs and driving in 3. The winner was Dan McLaws in relief. His record improves to 1-0. Kevin Comer took the loss. Picking up the save was Shae Simmons, his 3rd.

Salter had a game-changing home run in the bottom of the eighth. On a 1-0 changeup from Kevin Comer, he banged a solo home run to left-center, giving the Red Sox a 4-3 lead. It was the 6th home run of the year for Salter.

After the game, Boston manager matt atwell applauded the team's effort. "They busted their tails out there," he said.

With the win, Boston boosts its record to 10-7.
