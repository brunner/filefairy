Romero Blasts Red Sox, Rockies Take Game 5, 8-6
Already down in the World Series, the Colorado Rockies put on their best show, beating the Boston Red Sox by a final score of 8-6 today. Juan Romero earned player of the game honors, as he was 3-5 with a home run and a double, while adding 4 RBI and scoring once. Colorado now trails Boston 3-2 in the best-of-7 affair.

The Rockies were down to their final out when pinch hitter José Uribe turned a 1-0 fastball from Brad Boxberger into a 3-run home run. The walk-off home run made the Colorado Field crowd go wild.

"The name of the game is scoring runs," Romero said. "We did a pretty good job of that today."

The next game is scheduled for Thursday, November 2nd, 2023 at Fenway Park. Slated to start are Michael Matuella for the Rockies and Luiz Gohara for the Red Sox.
