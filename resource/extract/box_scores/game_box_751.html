The Rangers drop to 6-7 and continue their inconsistent start to the 2025 season.

Wilson had a game-changing single in the top of the tenth. On a 2-1 fastball from Kohl Stewart, he banged a run-scoring single to right, giving the Astros a 6-5 lead. It was the 12th hit of the year for Wilson.

Wilson told a Houston Observer reporter that he was "satisfied" with the win.
