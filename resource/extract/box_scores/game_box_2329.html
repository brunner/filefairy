The Cincinnati Reds did just enough to sneak past the Milwaukee Brewers, 7-6, at Miller Park. Cincinnati catcher Brian Reyes earned player of the game honors. Reyes had a game-changing home run in the top of the seventh. On a 1-1 fastball from Ryan Eades, he banged a 3-run home run to right, giving the Reds a 6-4 lead. It was the 2nd home run of the year for Reyes.

"It was a solid team effort," said Reyes after the game.
