Multi-HR Game for Windham in 5-4 Win
The Minnesota Twins beat the Seattle Mariners, 5-4 at Safeco Field, in part due to first baseman Jonathan Windham, who nabbed player of the game honors. He went 3 for 3 with 2 home runs and a walk.
