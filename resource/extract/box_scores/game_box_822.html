Diaz hits 2 home runs as Cubs Squeeze Past Milwaukee
The Chicago Cubs eked out a 6-5 win versus the Milwaukee Brewers at Miller Park. Nobody did more than first baseman Lewin Diaz, who captured player of the game honors. Jonathan Gates picked up the save, his 1st. The Cubs raised their record to 11-6.

Diaz had a game-changing home run in the top of the seventh. On a 1-0 fastball from Gabriel Ynoa, he banged a 3-run home run to left, giving the Cubs a 6-3 lead. It was the 6th home run of the year for Diaz.

"We put it together today and came out with the win," Diaz said in postgame remarks. "That's the main thing."
