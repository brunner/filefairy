Dodgers Win Again, Extend Series Lead to 2-0
Cincinnati right fielder Eric Sánchez pounded Los Angeles Dodgers pitchers at Great American Ball Park, but it was not enough to keep the Reds from falling, 7-5 in Game 2 of the Division Series. Sánchez went 2 for 3 with 2 home runs and a walk and was named player of the game for his efforts, even as he saw the Reds slip to a 2-0 deficit in the best-of-5 series.

Had Dan Eaton not delivered a key base hit in the top of the seventh, the outcome might have been different. Instead, with two down and a runner on 2nd, Eaton hit a run-scoring single. That made the score 6-5, in favor of the Dodgers.

Eaton said afterward that his team has grown over the course of the season, adding: "It feels good when a plan comes together."

The next game is set to be played on Wednesday, October 16th, 2024 at Dodger Stadium. Slated to start are Ken Richards for Cincinnati and Mario Ramírez for Los Angeles.
