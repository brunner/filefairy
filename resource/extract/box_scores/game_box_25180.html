Twins Even With Rays After Home Win
The Minnesota Twins and the Tampa Bay Rays will take the Division Series to a decisive 5th game. In a must-win situation today, the Twins beat the Rays 6-3 at Target Field to force the series into what will essentially be a one-game playoff. Minnesota right-hander Beau Burrows was named player of the game in the win after he threw 8 innings allowing 3 runs.

The Twins beat the Rays in part due to timely hitting. Case in point: the two-out home run Gavin Lux produced in the bottom of the fourth. Batting against Tampa Bay pitcher Enyerbeth Lugo, Lux gave his team a 4-0 lead when he hit a grand slam.

"Throwing strikes was the main thing," Burrows said.

The next game is set to be played on Sunday, October 20th, 2024 at Tropicana Field. The scheduled starters for the game are Jose Fernandez for Minnesota and Josh Sawyer for Tampa Bay.
