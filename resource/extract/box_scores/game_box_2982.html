Yelich hits 2 home runs as Astros Squeeze Past Oakland
The Houston Astros turned back the Oakland Athletics, 5-4, at Minute Maid Park. Christian Yelich was named player of the game for his fine performance. He was 2 for 3 with 2 home runs and a walk. Yelich had a game-changing home run in the bottom of the sixth. On a 1-1 fastball from Tim Cooney, he banged a 2-run home run to center, giving the Astros a 5-4 lead. It was the 7th home run of the year for Yelich.

"I just try to see the ball and put a good swing on it," said Yelich.
